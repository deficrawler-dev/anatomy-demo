From MRI Headaches to HOLOLUX

How a Neurosurgeon and the Solana Community Are Building the Future of Medical Imaging

Surgeons dislike VR headsets. They break workflow, force you to strap on bulky gear, and leave patients unable to see their own scans.

Dr. Galal A. (@galalnsg), a practicing Ivy League neurosurgeon, saw this frustration firsthand. Passionate about both medicine and AI, he set out to create something entirely new: HOLOLUX, the world’s first AI-powered glasses-free 3D medical imaging platform.

A New Way to See Inside the Human Body

  • AI super-resolution that sharpens MRI and CT scans
  • Infrared hand-tracking for direct interaction
  • Autostereoscopic 3D displays—no headset required

With HOLOLUX, any MRI or CT scan can be uploaded and instantly visualized in 3D. Stand in front of the screen and the scan appears as a hologram in the air—fully interactive. Patients finally see their condition in three dimensions.

How the Solana Community Stepped In

Tech evangelist Robert Scoble noticed the project. Using @BagsApp royalty sharing powered by @MeteoraAG, the community launched a coin that funnels trading fees directly into HOLOLUX development.

HOLOLUX shows what happens when medicine, AI, and crypto intersect—life-saving potential with global community momentum.
