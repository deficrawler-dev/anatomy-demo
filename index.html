Why We’re Raising Funds for HOLOLUX

We’re a community of supporters—independent from the company—spotlighting a medical technology we believe deserves to exist in every hospital. Through BagsApp, on-chain royalties from trading are directed toward HOLOLUX’s continued research and engineering. Think of it as a crypto-native fundraiser that helps pay for software milestones, hardware tests, and deployment.

How Community Funding Works

  • Launched on BagsApp with royalties earmarked to a development treasury.
  • Supporters help by holding or trading; fees accumulate transparently on-chain.
  • Progress updates and discussion happen in the open community.
  • This site promotes the fundraiser—not a coin “from the company.”

What Your Support Accelerates
